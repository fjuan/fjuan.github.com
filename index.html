The story

I moved from Cordoba to Madrid and my mother wrote me a cookbook with simple and delicious recipes. As I couldn't afford to loose it, I published its recipes to Blogger.

Some months later, she asked me to set up a website for her so she could publish more recipes... without doubt, we decided to share the site and before it had too much traction, we moved it to its own domain: recetasdemama.es
